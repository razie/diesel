The trust levels are:

  • Public - complete faith, all child's activity is public and he/she can modify any information without supervision

  • ParentModerated - child's activity is moderated by you (nothing will change until you aprove)

Notification levels are:

  • Everything - notify you be email of any activity of the child
  • Content - notify you when the child modifies any actual content
  • None - no notifications of the child's activity
